Andrew Stanish

Curler

About

Andrew is a talented, Winnipeg youth curler, and is well known throughout Glenlawn Collegiate. Andrew has made the news dozens of times, with "Just go curl, it's so fun" being his go-to motto. Andrew is a member of the highly-revered Heather Curling Club, which is one of the best Curling facilities in the world, he and a group of highly-specialized curlers recieve daily instruction from olympic-gold medalist Jill Officer. “I love the intricacies and small details that go into having a good delivery, good sweeping,” Andrew said in his latest interview. Andrew looks up to 5-time olympic medalist Rachel Homan as his role model, and he is close to reaching her level. When Thomas Gan, which is one of his close friends, was asked "Do you think Andrew will make the Olympic teams one day?" Thomas replied with absolute confidence "Oh, he's going to win a gold medal one day." Andrew's previous interviews can be found below.
